**Source:** Tech Breakthroughs | **Date:** April 2, 2026

- **Global Impact:** This breakthrough paves the way for electric vehicles with unlimited range, grid-scale storage for renewable energy, and uninterrupted power for critical infrastructure and healthcare systems.
